AI Showcase 2024 Recap Apr 26, 2024, 11:26 AM Share AI Showcase Recap: A Celebration of Student Innovations Event Overview Vanderbilt Data Science was thrilled to host this year’s AI Showcase, marking another milestone in our journey of technological exploration and innovation. This event provided a stage for students to demonstrate their groundbreaking AI projects and allowed attendees to witness the practical applications of advanced data science. Interactive Presentations and Exceptional Turnout The AI Showcase featured diverse presentations from our talented students. Each session offered a 30-minute deep dive into AI-driven projects, ranging from environmental solutions to educational tools. The event attracted a large audience of over 60 attendees, including students, faculty, staff, researchers, and industry professionals, all eager to learn about and discuss the latest developments from students in artificial intelligence. The collective enthusiasm contributed to a dynamic atmosphere where ideas and insights flourished. Comprehensive Range of Projects This year’s showcase was particularly notable for the breadth and quality of projects presented. A total of 18 innovative projects were on display, demonstrating the wide-ranging applications of AI across various disciplines. The projects spanned several key categories, showcasing how data science and AI can drive significant advancements across different sectors. Winners and Their Innovative Projects The event concluded with the recognition of three outstanding projects, which were selected for their exceptional creativity, technical excellence, and potential impact. The awards ceremony was a highlight of the day, celebrating the hard work and success of our students. 1st Place: $1000 – DiffWater Team DiffWater utilized equivariant graph neural networks and score-based diffusion models to model water molecule distributions around protein surfaces. Their innovative approach could significantly impact biochemical research. 2nd Place: $500 – ChatEDU ChatEDU, created by a visionary team of developers, aims to revolutionize educational interactions through a meticulously designed online platform, enhancing both teaching and learning experiences. 3rd Place: $200 – Cleft Palate Project The Cleft Palate Project team developed an AI-driven tool that analyzes speech patterns to provide valuable feedback for speech therapy techniques, supporting individuals with speech difficulties. AI in the Community The showcase was not only about presenting projects but also about building community. Attendees enjoyed numerous networking opportunities, engaging with innovators and experts over refreshments that kept the conversations flowing.
