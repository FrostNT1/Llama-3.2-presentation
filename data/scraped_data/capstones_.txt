Capstones Capstone projects at Vanderbilt Data Science serve as the pinnacle of our students’ academic journey, offering them the chance to apply their skills in real-world settings. By partnering with industry leaders, we create opportunities for students to tackle complex, practical challenges. These collaborations not only bridge the gap between classroom learning and professional application but also help shape the future of data science, one project at a time. On this page What is a Capstone Project? A Capstone Project is the culmination of the Data Science journey at Vanderbilt. It provides an opportunity to apply advanced skills in a real-world setting, addressing challenges presented by industry partners. This experience bridges the gap between academic learning and professional application, allowing participants to solve complex data problems and deliver impactful results. Capstone Structure and Timeline Overview: A Capstone Project follows a structured timeline, ensuring participants can fully apply their data science skills while meeting the expectations of industry partners. The process typically spans a semester and includes key phases that guide participants from project selection to final deliverables. Capstones can happen either in the Fall Semester or Spring Semester. Key Phases ~1 month prior to semesterParticipants begin by choosing a project that aligns with their interests and career goals. The primary task in this phase is clearly defining the problem statement and identifying and securing the necessary data and/or tools to solve the problem. During the semester (Fall or Spring)The core of the Capstone Project involves executing the planned analysis or solution development. This phase requires regular check-ins with advisors and industry partners to ensure progress and address any challenges. End of semester/final exam weekThe project culminates with the submission of final deliverables, which include at a minimum a comprehensive report, a GitHub repository, and a presentation summarizing the project’s problem statement and outcomes Where do Capstone projects come from? Capstone Projects can arise from many different sources. Our primary goal is for you to work on something that is energizing and exciting, such that you look forward to the work and want to talk about it with others.
