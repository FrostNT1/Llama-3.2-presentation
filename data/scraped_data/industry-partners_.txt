Industry Partners At Vanderbilt Data Science, we collaborate with industry leaders across various sectors to provide real-world experience through capstones and internships. Our partnerships bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical application, allowing students to work alongside professionals on impactful projects. Together, we’re shaping the future of data science and preparing the next generation of innovators. On this page Who does Vanderbilt Data Science partner with? Our partnerships span a diverse range of industries, offering our students the chance to engage in impactful projects with leaders like Bridgestone, Asurion, and HCA. Whether it’s working on AI-driven insights with Symtrain.ai or tackling data challenges with tech giants like NVIDIA, our collaborations cover a broad spectrum of applications. From AI-driven analysis with HCAc to sports analytics with Nashville SC, each partnership provides unique opportunities to apply data science in meaningful ways, ensuring our students gain valuable, hands-on experience in the field. Our capstone projects are a key way in which we achieve this, offering both our students and industry partners the opportunity to collaborate on impactful, data-driven projects. Here’s how we work together to create value. Our industry partners provide real-world challenges for our students through capstone projects. These projects allow students to apply their data science skills to solve pressing issues in fields ranging from healthcare to finance, sports, and beyond. Experts from our partner companies often engage with our students directly through guest lectures and co-taught courses. This collaboration brings practical insights and cutting-edge industry knowledge into the classroom, enhancing the learning experience. Many of our industry partners offer internships and job opportunities to our students, helping them transition from academic life to professional careers. Companies like Asurion, Nissan USA, and HCA have hired our graduates, reinforcing the value of our partnership. What is a Capstone Project? In the second year of the M.S. in Data Science program, students undertake a capstone project. These projects allow students to apply their skills to real-world problems provided by our industry partners. They work on defining a problem, analyzing relevant data, and delivering a solution over the course of a semester​​.
