Leveraging AI for Fan Sentiment Analysis Aug 13, 2024, 7:36 PM Share Nashville SC – Leveraging AI for Fan Sentiment Analysis In collaboration with Vanderbilt Data Science, Nashville SC undertook a project aimed at enhancing their understanding of fan sentiment through the analysis of social media discussions. By applying advanced AI techniques, the project provided detailed insights into how fans perceive the club compared to other professional sports teams in Nashville. These findings have been instrumental in guiding Nashville SC’s strategies for engaging with their fanbase more effectively. Fan Sentiment Analysis Presentation The project results were presented by two data science students who showcased the methods used to extract and analyze fan sentiment from Reddit, a popular platform for sports discussions. This analysis gave Nashville SC a clearer picture of public opinion and helped identify key areas where fan engagement could be improved. Project Highlights: In the fall of 2023, two students utilized Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA) and Generative AI to analyze over 3,600 Reddit threads discussing Nashville SC and other local sports teams.
