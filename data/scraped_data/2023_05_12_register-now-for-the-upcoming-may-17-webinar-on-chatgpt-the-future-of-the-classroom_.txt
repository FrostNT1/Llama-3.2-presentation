Register now for the upcoming May 17 webinar on “ChatGPT: The Future of the Classroom” May 12, 2023, 12:43 PM Share Discover the exciting potential of ChatGPT in education during our upcoming webinar, “ChatGPT: the Future of the Classroom.” Join us on Wednesday, May 17, from 11:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m., as we explore how ChatGPT is revolutionizing educational experiences. Our panel of experts from Vanderbilt University includes Dr. Douglas Schmidt, Cornelius Vanderbilt Professor of Engineering, Professor of Computer Science, Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering, and Associate Chair of Computer Science; Dr. Jesse Spencer-Smith, Chief Data Scientist and Interim Director of the Data Science Institute; and Dr. Jules White, Associate Dean for Strategic Learning Programs and Associate Professor of Computer Science. The panel of experts will delve into the various ways ChatGPT can enhance learning. From interactive games to virtual laboratories and responsive discussion forums, ChatGPT offers instructors powerful tools to create engaging educational materials. This webinar will go beyond its impact on student test-taking and explore how ChatGPT can support independent learning, personal growth, and retraining opportunities. This virtual event is open to the Vanderbilt community, including alumni, students, and parents.
